Shipping Policy

Our orders are shipped within 2 working days of the order, and delivered in 2-7 days from shipping. We ship through leading courier services and aggregators, and you can track your order status after it is shipped. In the event where we are unable to deliver any items in your order or your entire order, be assured that you will not be charged for the item/order or you shall be refunded for the full item/order cost.

  • Our shipping and delivery lead times may vary by destination.
  • We ship on all the weekdays excluding public holidays.
  • We only ship through reputed courier agencies to ensure that your order reaches you in the fastest time and safe condition.
  • While we try and ship all your items in your order together but this might not be always possible due to mix up in the courier agencies or product characteristics.
  • If you believe that the product is not in good condition, or if packaging is torn or tampered, before accepting the delivery, please refuse to take delivery of the goods and reach us at our Customer Care.
  • All the items will be shipped with an invoice mentioning the price as per Indian Tax Regulations.
