Cancellation Policy

Cancellation by flavido.com

While we will make every effort to fulfill the orders you have placed, in certain cases Flavido.com reserves the right to cancel orders in the following cases:-

  • When products goes out of stock with our supplier or seller or partner brand.
  • When there is limited Quantity available for Purchase.
  • When we have restrictions on the quantity or number or orders As per T&C.
  • When the address and/or contact details provided by you are inaccurate or insufficient.
  • When our delivery partners do not service your location. Currently we have limited supply in the North East and Jammu & Kashmir.

Cancellation by the Customer

You as a customer can cancel your order anytime till the order has not been processed by us by calling our customer service. In such a case we will refund any payments already made by you for the order. We will not be able to cancel those orders that have already been processed and shipped by us. If we suspect any fraudulent transaction by any customer or any transaction which defies the terms & conditions of using the website, we at our sole discretion could cancel such orders. We will maintain a negative list of all fraudulent transactions and customers and would deny access to them or cancel any orders placed by them.

Return & Refunds

Orders once placed with us cannot be modified or clubbed with any other order. We use the best packaging methods for securing the orders from any damage as well as the services of the best shipping partners to ensure that the orders reach you in good condition. Though we make sure that the order is undamaged, we do not take any responsibility for the damages caused to your order while in transit. If you have received a damaged/ defective book or it does not comply with the specifications as per your original order, you are required to get in touch with us by dropping an email to support@flavido.com. If possible send us the image of the order as an attachment with the email. You can get your order replaced only once without any extra charge and we will send you the replacement only when we have received the book(s) back from you. We will take the returned product back with us and issue a credit note for the value of the return product(s). All the refunds either through cancellation or returns will be credited to your account on the Site. This can be used to pay your subsequent shopping bills. Please note that the shipping charges are non-refundable.
